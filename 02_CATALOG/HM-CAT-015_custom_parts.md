# Custom Part: NMT-MP-001 (Motor Plate)

> [!CRITICAL]
> **The linchpin of the design.** This plate adapts the G30's "Double-D" axle to the 2040 frame. Failure here means a wheel falls off.

## 1. Geometry

*   **Material:** 6061-T6 Aluminum (Assessment: Sufficient) or 304 Stainless (Overkill but durable).
*   **Thickness:** **6.0mm** (Match axle flat length).
*   **Overall Size:** ~80mm x 80mm (Triangular/Trapezoidal gusset shape).

## 2. The "Double-D" Output Slot (CDR-2)

*   **Shaft Diameter:** 12.0mm
*   **Flat-to-Flat:** 10.0mm
*   **Corner Radii:** **R2.0mm (Min)** in the corners of the slot.
    *   *Why?* Sharp corners create stress risers that will crack under 30Nm torque cycles.

## 3. Frame Interface

*   **Bolt Pattern:** 4x M5 Clearance Holes (5.5mm).
*   **Spacing:** 20mm grid to match V-Slot end profile (tapped holes) or side T-slots.
*   **Fasteners:** M5 x 15mm Socket Head Cap Screws.

## 4. Fabrication Notes

*   **Process:** Waterjet or Laser Cut.
*   **Tolerance:** +/- 0.1mm on the Axle Slot. (Loose = Slop/Wear, Tight = Won't fit).
*   **Post-Process:** Deburr edges. Ream slot if necessary for snug fit.

## 5. Quantity

*   **Total:** 4 Plates (1 per wheel).
*   **Spares:** Order 6 (2 spares recommended).
